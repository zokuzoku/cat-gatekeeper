How It Works

A tiny rule, enforced by a very serious cat.

01

Choose websites

Use the default social sites or add your own domains, one per line.

02

Set your limit

Pick how many active minutes are allowed before a break starts.

03

Meet the cat

When time is up, the cat appears and keeps the page covered until the countdown ends.

Privacy First

No accounts. No ads. No data collection.

Settings and timers are stored in your browser. Cat Gatekeeper does not collect, sell, or transmit your browsing data.
